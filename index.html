LIBRARY MANAGEMENT SYSTEM

     The library management deals in management of records. The records include books which are required by different students and staffs of an institution. Maintaining these records manually becomes difficult thus we are providing a computerized backend for such a system.With the growing information technology industry, automation of their system and management is desired by all kind of commercial enterprises. As the name suggest Library Management System will deal in the entire requirement needed for managing the activities of an institute library. It will deal with the process of maintaining data about the books and many other things which are taking place in the library such as the issue, searching ,return of the books, adding new books, deleting books,viewing fines etc. So LMS (Library management System) helps in easy functioning of the library and saves a lot of time. It even ensures no data is missing and handles every record efficiently and effectively.
